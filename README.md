# 🌿 KeenKeeper

> *Your personal shelf of meaningful connections. Browse, tend, and nurture the relationships that matter most.*

---

## 📖 About

**KeenKeeper** is a friendship relationship manager that helps you stay intentional about the people in your life. Set contact goals, track your last interaction, and get notified when a friendship needs attention — so no one slips through the cracks.

---

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 1. 🎯 Smart Friend Status Tracking
Every friend card displays a real-time status — **Overdue**, **Almost Due**, or **On-Track** — based on your last contact date vs your personal goal. Color-coded badges make it instantly clear who needs your attention.

### 2. ⚡ Quick Check-In Logger
From any friend's detail page, log a **Call**, **Text**, or **Video** interaction in one click. Each check-in is timestamped and saved to a persistent timeline — so your interaction history is never lost, even after a page refresh.

### 3. 📊 Friendship Analytics
The Stats page visualizes your interaction habits with a **Recharts pie chart**, breaking down your logged interactions by type (Call / Text / Video). Filter your timeline by interaction type to review your history at a glance.

---

## 📸 Pages

- **Home** — Friend grid with status cards and summary stats
- **Friend Details** — Full profile, relationship goal, and quick check-in
- **Timeline** — Full interaction history
- **Stats** — Pie chart analytics of your communication habits

npm install

# Start the development server
npm run dev
